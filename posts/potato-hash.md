### Ingredients

- 2 white potatoes
- 2 sweet potatoes
- Butter
- Spices of your choice (the more the better)
  - Garlic powder
  - Oregano
  - Rosemary
  - Salt
  - Thyme

### Directions

- Preheat oven to 425
- Cut potatoes into 1/2" cubes and toss with oil and spices
- Put in a large glass dish
- Cook for 45-75 minutes depending on how big the cubes are
