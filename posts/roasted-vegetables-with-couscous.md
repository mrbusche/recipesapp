### Ingredients

- 2 white potatoes
- 2 sweet potatoes
- 1 onion
- 1 pepper
- 2-3 beets
- 5 cloves garlic, mined
- olive oil
- spices

### Directions

- Preheat oven to 450
- Cut everything into small cubes and cover with oil and spices
- Cook for 50 minutes, mixing a lot
- Server over couscous
