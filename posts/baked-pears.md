### Ingredients

- 2-3 ripe pears
- coconut oil
- coconut sugar
- cinnamon

### Directions

- Preheat oven to 425
- Peel pears and cut into 4 big pieces
- Place into 8x8 baking dish
- Baste with coconut oil
- Sprinkle cinnamon and sugar on top
