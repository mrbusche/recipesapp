### Ingredients

- 3 or 4 white potatoes
- Olive oil
- Spices of your choice (the more the better)
  - Garlic powder
  - Oregano
  - Rosemary
  - Salt
  - Thyme

### Directions

- Preheat oven to 425
- Cut potatoes into quarters and toss with oil and spices
- Put in a large glass dish
- Cook for 45 minutes
