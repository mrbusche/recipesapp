### Ingredients

- 1 1/2 cups flour
- 1 Tbsp baking powder
- 1/2 tsp salt
- 2 Tbsp sugar or maple syrup
- 1 cup milk
- 1/2 cup water
- 2 Tbsp canola oil

### Directions

- Mix dry ingredients in large bowl
- Add wet ingredients and stir until combined
- Drop about 1/3 cup of batter onto a skillet

Pancakes freeze well
