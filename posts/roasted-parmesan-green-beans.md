### Ingredients

- Green Beans
- 2 Tbsp olive oil
- 1/4 tsp garlic powder
- 1 1/2 tsp parmesan

### Directions

- Preheat oven to 425
- Lay green beans on baking sheet and drizzle with oil and garlic powder
- Cook for 15 minutes, shaking pan after 10 minutes
- Remove from oven and sprinkle with parmesan
