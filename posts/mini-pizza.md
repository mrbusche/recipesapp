### Ingredients

- 4" tortillas
- pizza sauce
- toppings of your choice
  - Mozzarella cheese
  - peppers
  - pepperoni

### Directions

- Preheat oven to 450
- Put pizza sauce on tortilla
- Add toppings
- Cook for 8 minutes
