### Ingredients

- 3 c almond milk
- 3/4 c cornstarch
- 3 tsp flax seeds
- 1.5 tsp baking powder
- 3 tsp cinnamon
- 6 tsp maple syrup
- 3 tsp vanilla
- 18-24 slices french bread

### Directions

- Whisk together all ingredients
- Dip bread into mix
- Cook on a griddle until golden brown
